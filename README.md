 `Overview` 

Court.io is a native iOS and watchOS app designed to revolutionize how racket sport players track their matches. Starting with squash and expanding to paddle, tennis, and pickleball, the app enables real-time scoring through Apple Watch during gameplay, comprehensive health tracking via HealthKit, and detailed match analytics.

Whether you're playing a casual match or competing seriously, Court.io provides seamless score tracking with your Apple Watch on your wrist, eliminating the need to interrupt play. The app captures your heart rate, calories burned, and match duration automatically, giving you insights into both your performance and fitness.

`Key Features Currently in development (v1.0)`

`Dual Scoring Modes`

Primary: Apple Watch scoring during active play
Alternative: iPhone dedicated scorer mode

`Health Tracking Integration`

Real-time heart rate monitoring
Calories burned calculation
Match duration and intensity metrics
HealthKit workout session recording

`Match Management`

Complete match history with detailed statistics
Opponent tracking and records
Court/location tagging
Win/loss analytics


`Offline-First Architecture`

Full functionality without internet connection
Automatic cloud sync when online
Cross-device synchronization

`User Authentication`

Email/password authentication
Apple Sign In support


`Technology Stack`
- Frontend: SwiftUI
- Backend: Firebase for cloud storage, real-time database, and user authentication.
- Language: Swift
- Persistence/DB: SwiftData, Firestore
- Frameworks: WatchConnectivity, Foundation, HealthKit
